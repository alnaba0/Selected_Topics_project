Upload an image. It gets fingerprinted with SHA-256, classified by a CNN, and the result is mined into a tamper-evident block. Nothing about the stored record can change without breaking every hash after it.

01

Hash

The raw bytes are hashed with SHA-256 to produce a unique fingerprint of the exact file submitted.

02

Classify

A pretrained ResNet18 CNN returns a top label and confidence score for the image.

03

Mine

Hash + label + confidence are packed into a block, linked to the previous block's hash, and mined.
